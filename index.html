About Me

Future goals

Hello, my name is Mahib Rahman, and I'm a senior at West Hempstead High School. I am excited to major in Computer Science in college and pursue a career in that field. Furthermore, meteorology is a side interest of mine, and I intend to minor in it.

Hobbies

One hobby that I have is that I love playing my viola and I have 10 years of experience. I also love watching soccer and playing sports like tennis and badminton. Furthermore, listening to BTS and watching K-Dramas are some other things that I enjoy.

Family and Background

I come from a Bengali-American household with a family of five. I have two sisters named Mahia and Lamia and they, along with my parents, always gave me the best advice. My whole family keeps me grounded and they find ways to improve myself as a person.
